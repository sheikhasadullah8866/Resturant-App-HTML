Chicken Fajita Pizza

A chicken fajita pizza is a popular fusion dish that blends the sizzling, smoky flavors of Tex-Mex fajitas with the cheesy comfort of a classic Italian pizza. It features seasoned strips of grilled or pan-fried chicken with tender bell peppers and onions, all baked on a pizza crust with a tangy base sauce and a generous layer of melted cheese.
